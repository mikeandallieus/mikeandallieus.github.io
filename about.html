About Us

Allie and Mike met in the fall of 2015. At the time, both felt like they were in transition: Mike had just moved to New York and Allie was contemplating business school. But it didn't take long for these two to realize that any future life changes were going to include each other. Within four months they were traveling to Italy and six months after that, they decided to live together, moving into their first apartment, across from Prospect Park, on their one year anniversary.

In November 2017, Mike proposed to Allie with a crossword puzzle filled with inside jokes and sweet nothings. Allie and Mike have loved making a home with each other, and of course, Bruce, their beloved black cat. They are foodies, real estate geeks, television aficionados, and they love throwing gatherings for their friends. They both love their families very much and overjoyed to merge them in June 2018.
