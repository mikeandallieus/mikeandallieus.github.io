How We Met

In the summer of 2015, Mike had just moved to Brooklyn to pursue a new career and Allie was about to embark on what would ultimately be a failed attempt an MBA. They were each on an adventure; little did they know, an even bigger one was on the horizon.

They met at the end of August of that year through OKCupid. Mike sent Allie a message which included a joke about Jewish Texans having Longhorn Menorahs. Allie immediately agreed to meet him for a drink.

Exactly 1 year later Allie, Mike, and their cat, Bruce, moved into their place on Ocean Avenue. They both feel immensely grateful for the relationship they've formed, and are excited to share it with everyone on their wedding day.
